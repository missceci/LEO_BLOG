Places I have visited



Chicago

I visited Chicago in 2023

Chicago is an excellent place. It was a very fun experience, with interesting museums, tall buildings, and more. I especially liked the activities where you could go to the highest point of a building and look down. It was really cool. And the deep dish pizza there was delicious. I also saw the Bean which was a really pretty sculpture that I saw and I could reccomend. Overall this was a really good trip.




Georgia

I visited Georgia in 2021

Georgia was a fun experience. I might not have remembered everything I did or saw, but what I do remember, it was amazing. I remember seeing the fourth of july fireworks there, which were amazing, and saw a coca cola factory. They were both fun experiences, but my favorite activity was definetely the world's biggest aquarium that we went to there. It was humongus, and spectacular, I would stroungly reccomend this. This was an excellent trip.




Japan

I visited Japan in 2024

Chicago and Georgia were fun experiences, but by far my favorite place that I visited was Japan. The culture, the shrines, the food, the anime, everthing was unique and interesting. The activities I did there was exploring Japan's shrines, and doing activities such as teamLab, and just exploring the cities. This trip was long and included a lot of walking, and a lot of shrines, but was worth it.

Review On The Movies

Godzilla Minus One

There are many reasons I like this movie. It has action, a good story, and interesting characters. It did not disappoint, and was a good watch. I would reccomend this movie to people who like a good story, and don't mind there being more story than action. It was well worth the visit.

Review On My Favorite Books

This is my favortie book, which is the fan favorite, Harry Potter. I like it because of the magic, action, story and characters. It was a very enjoyable read. I would reccomend this to people who like sorcery and a good, yet long story.

This is my second favorite book, Percy Jackson. It has interesting characters, settings, and good action and plot. It was a spectacular read with twists and turns. I would reccomend this to someone who likes greek mythology and a lot of action.
